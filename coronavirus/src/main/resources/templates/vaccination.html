Vaccination instructions

When inoculated

(1) Wear a mask during the whole process and line up in an orderly manner according to the mark of the vaccination point.

(2) to take the initiative to truthfully provide the doctor with their health status and other information, truthfully fill in the informed consent.

After inoculation

(1) Stay for 30 minutes after vaccination and leave without symptoms.

(2) Keep the inoculation site dry on the day of inoculation, avoid scratching the inoculation site with your hand, and avoid strenuous exercise.

(3) Within 24 to 48 hours after vaccination, the recipient should observe the reaction of the vaccination area, whether there are symptoms such as redness, swelling, headache, fever, etc. If there are symptoms, they should seek medical attention in time and report to the vaccination unit.

(4) Within one week after vaccination, avoid contact with personal known allergies and common allergens, try not to drink alcohol, do not eat spicy stimulation and seafood food, it is recommended to eat light diet and drink more water.
